BusyQuizzy



BusyQuizzy is a quiz hosting website, with the purpose of being a newly found source of fun and entertainment. The goal was to create engaging website for users to create quizzes and take quizzes. All quizzes are be multiple choice with four answers, one of these answers being the correct one.

Users are able to earn points by answering questions correctly, and they can use those points to purchase Quiz Helpers. The Quiz Helpers assist the user in choosing the correct answer. Users must create an account in order to see their balance and make purchases in the shop page.

codersPlatform



The purpose of codersPlatform is to provide a social media platform aimed towards anybody with a creative mind, but primarily aimed towards developers. Plenty of people come up with ideas every day for an app, a project, a device, etc.

Users can create a profile where they can upload a profile picture, their username, email, and other personal information. With the creation of an account, users can log in and create posts that are shown in the public feed. Users can also search for other accounts within the system and view that account and their posts.
